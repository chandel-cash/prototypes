About the Afterpay Media Network

The Afterpay Media Network (AMN) is a service that allows advertisers to reach consumers with relevant offers based on their shopping habits. When you make a purchase at a business that uses Square and receive a digital receipt, your transaction data may be used to help deliver more relevant advertising to you.

By opting out, you are requesting that Square not share your personal information with third parties for targeted advertising purposes through the Afterpay Media Network.
